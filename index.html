My Story

我的故事

"I grew up in Vietnam and chose to build my career across borders — where markets, cultures and languages meet."

「我在越南長大，並選擇跨越國界打造職涯 —— 在市場、文化與語言交會之處。」

Fluent in Vietnamese, English and Mandarin, I came to Taiwan for an International MBA at National Cheng Kung University, where I was honored as a Top 10% Outstanding Student. Along the way I founded a sustainable startup, represented my university on global stages, and shipped products that solve real business problems.

Today I work in international marketing & sales and market research, focused on the Vietnam market — owning competitor analysis, consumer insight and go-to-market strategy. My edge: I pair classic marketing instincts with fluency in generative AI, using it to research faster, prototype ideas and turn insight into action. My mission is to be the bridge that helps brands grow between Southeast Asia and the world.

精通越南語、英語與中文，我來到台灣攻讀成功大學 IMBA，並榮獲前 10% 傑出學生獎。一路上，我創辦了永續品牌、代表學校站上國際舞台，也打造了解決真實商業問題的產品。

如今，我從事國際行銷業務與市場調研，專注越南市場 —— 負責競品分析、消費者洞察與市場進入策略。我的優勢：將傳統行銷直覺與生成式 AI 能力結合，用它加速研究、快速驗證點子，並將洞察化為行動。我的使命，是成為連結東南亞與世界、助品牌成長的橋樑。
